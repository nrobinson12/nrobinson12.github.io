Booking Software

Web Development - Ctrl V
Project Overview
While I was interning at the local VR Arcade, I was tasked with the responsibility of simplifying and converting their existing Booking Software, so that future developers can have an easier time learning it’s ins and outs. You can check out the current software, used by thousands across Canada, that I contributed heavily to here.
The Specifics
When I joined the team, their online booking software was developed by another developer that had since gone, and they created it with Reactjs in Typescript. To new interns, this is a steep learning curve and can eat into their 4-month term with Ctrl V. So, in order to make their software simpler to maintain, I decided to convert the Typescript to just plain Javascript. This will lessen training time for new hires and will make it easier to alter for existing hires.
Working at the local VR Arcade startup that all my friends knew about was extremely fun. During my time there, I got to touch most of all the aspects of their platform. From developing new endpoints for retrieving data, to maintaining their online bookings, I learned a massive amount at this amazing company.
