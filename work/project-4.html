Full Stack Development - Houzz
Project Overview
After my first few beginner projects to get me familiar with their stack, Houzz tasked me with a relatively large project of implementing mentions/tags into their discussion board. On the Houzz platform, users are encouraged to share their home projects and post questions for other users to answer if they need any help. It is very popular, but users were asking for features they were accustomed to on other social media type sites, one of which being the ability to mention other users in comments or posts.
Scope
This was a large project mainly because there was no already existing code to support this feature, neither in the back end nor the front end. So, I got to work with many different full-time developers working on many different aspects of the platform! I was the main developer working on this new feature, with significant guidance from the full-time employees that have worked there for much longer.
With this project, I got to see a lot of inner workings of Houzz’s platform. I gained an immense amount of experience working with Reactjs, Javascript, PHP, Nodejs, GraphQL, and MySQL. I also got to be involved in the development process, getting a lot of feedback from multiple developers at a time. Thus, gaining experience working with and collaborating with other people toward a single goal.
